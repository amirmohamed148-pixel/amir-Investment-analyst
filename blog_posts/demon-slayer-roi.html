Demon Slayer ROI Analysis

Production Budget: ~$15M | Box Office: ~$480M | ROI ≈ 32×

For comparison: Avengers: Endgame ≈ 8×, Spider-Man: No Way Home ≈ 9×.

Investment Story

Demon Slayer leveraged strong IP, global distribution, merchandise, and streaming deals to generate massive ROI. Strategic marketing amplified revenue far beyond the box office.

This shows how small-budget anime movies can outperform Hollywood when combining cultural relevance, global reach, and smart monetization.
