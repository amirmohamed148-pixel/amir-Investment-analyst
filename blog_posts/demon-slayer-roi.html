Demon Slayer ROI Analysis

Production Budget: ~$15M | Box Office: ~$480M | ROI ≈ 32×

For comparison: Avengers: Endgame achieved ~8× ROI, Spider-Man: No Way Home ~9×.

Investment Story

Demon Slayer leveraged a strong existing IP and a strategic release schedule to maximize global audience reach while keeping marketing costs low. Licensing, merchandising, and streaming deals provided additional revenue streams.

This shows that even a relatively small-budget anime movie can achieve extraordinary returns when combining cultural relevance, global distribution, and smart monetization strategies. Anime films are becoming a high-leverage opportunity for investors seeking outsized ROI.
