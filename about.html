Who I Am

I am an investment and financial analyst who approaches business like an artist — combining creative vision with technical rigor. I learned to tell stories visually and translate ideas into models that investors and boards can act on.

My path began in internships and early roles where I learned credit, risk and client-facing analysis. Over time I focused on real estate, developing pricing strategies, payment structures and acquisition models. I also work across renewable energy, healthcare and fintech — building feasibility studies, valuations, and investor materials.

Beyond numbers I bring design and presentation skills: polished reports, clear dashboards, and investor-ready decks. I value clarity, humility, and results-focused collaboration.
